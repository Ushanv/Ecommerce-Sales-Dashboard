# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze e-commerce sales data and generate actionable insights on sales performance, customer behavior, and profit trends using Power BI.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

* Order ID, Order Date
* Product Category & Sub-category
* Sales, Profit, Quantity
* Customer details
* Payment Mode
* Region & State

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* DAX (Measures & Calculations)
* Excel / CSV Dataset

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

* 📈 Sales & Profit Trend Analysis (Monthly)
* 🗺️ Region-wise & State-wise Sales Analysis
* 🛍️ Category & Sub-category Performance
* 💳 Payment Mode Analysis
* 👥 Customer Contribution Analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, AOV)
* Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Time)
* Bar Charts, Pie Charts, Line Charts
* Drill-down functionality for deeper insights

## 🚀 Key Insights

* 📌 Total Sales reached ₹3.37 Lakhs, showing steady business performance
* 📌 Maharashtra contributes the highest sales (~₹1.02 Lakhs), making it the primary revenue-driving state
* 📌 Electronics category dominates with ~49% of total sales, indicating strong demand
* 📌 Sales peak in January, March, and November, highlighting seasonal buying trends
* 📌 Profit drops in mid-year months (May–July), suggesting pricing or cost inefficiencies
* 📌 Top customers contribute a significant share of revenue, indicating dependency on high-value customers

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Helps businesses identify high-performing products
* Supports decision-making for marketing strategies
* Enables tracking of sales trends and profitability
* Improves customer targeting and retention strategies

---
## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 📌 Focus marketing and inventory planning during high-sales months (Jan, Mar, Nov)
* 📌 Improve pricing strategy during low-profit months to reduce losses
* 📌 Expand business operations in Maharashtra to maximize revenue
* 📌 Promote Electronics category further as it drives the highest sales
* 📌 Introduce loyalty programs for top customers to retain high-value users
* 📌 Analyze cost structure in loss-making months to improve profitability
